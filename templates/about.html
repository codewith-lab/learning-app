About the Team

Cat Body Language Tutorial

This website is designed to help cat owners and enthusiasts better understand feline body language. By learning to interpret your cat's signals, you can improve your relationship with your pet and ensure their wellbeing.

Features:

  • Comprehensive tutorials on different emotional states
  • Interactive scenarios to practice recognition
  • Quizzes to test your knowledge
  • Visual guides with detailed explanations
  • Cute anime-style illustrations

Technical Details:

  • Backend: Flask (Python)
  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery
  • Styling: Bootstrap with custom anime-style CSS
  • Interactive elements: Custom JavaScript
  • Database: SQLite for quiz results
